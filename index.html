Space Ark: Survival — Interactive Web Mission

Space Ark: Survival is a browser-based interactive adventure that combines a science-fiction story with healthy daily habits. Players unlock each chapter by completing hydration and workout tasks, making the experience both entertaining and motivating.

How to Play

Start with the introductory mission, follow the AI prompts, and complete the water and workout tasks each day. As you progress, new chapters unlock and the story advances through atmospheric sci-fi scenes and mission reports.

Why It Works

This game encourages mindful hydration and movement in a playful format. It is designed to be easy to access in the browser, responsive on phones and tablets, and friendly for users looking for a short interactive wellness challenge.
